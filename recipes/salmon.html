Pan-Seared Salmon

There’s nothing better to cook up as a middle-of-the-week, savory treat like a simple salmon filet. It’s hearty and packed with nutrients that pairs well with just about any carb and any vegetable. Similar to many fish filets, they can have an impeccably quick defrost time mdash; meaning you can think of dinner at noon and have it neatly plated by evening.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fillet(s), whatever you think is a reasonable amount
